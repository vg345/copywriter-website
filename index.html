Let the world know your brand.

It's a simple fact that people can't do business with you if they don't know you exist. There's no point to a fantastic product if nobody's using it. Marketing and advertising are what spread word about your awesome brand, so that more people can get to know about you. Which means in the final analysis, that you're a very happy company with lots of profit rolling in if you get the marketing right.
